Pastor Mitchell

District Elder Malcolm Mitchell is the Pastor of Fellowship Mission Church. His preaching ministry was birthed at age 18 under the discipleship of Regional Apostle HA Moultrie in the Mission Church of Christ. Elder Mitchell along with a group of his struggling high school students, was led by the Holy Ghost to start a Bible study in the city of Boston that quickly grew into a worshipping congregation. To God be the Glory.

Elder Mitchell is happily married to his wife of 18 years – Sister and affectionately known as First lady Carla Mitchell. They have 3 wonderful children, Malcolm jr, Matthew and Mia. Pastor Mitchell holds a Master’s degree in education and a certificate of advanced graduate studies in educational administration – he currently works in the Boston public school system as a teacher and wrestling coach. He is also Pastoring Fellowship Mission Church full time as well and looking for the Lord to use the church to Fellowship Believers into the Body of Christ.
